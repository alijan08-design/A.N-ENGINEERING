Our Story

About A.N Power

15+ years of engineering excellence, serving Karachi and Pakistan with reliable power solutions.

Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

A.N Power Engineering & Services was established on March 5, 2009, with a singular vision: to provide Pakistan's businesses and communities with reliable, high-quality engineering solutions they can depend on.

Starting from our roots in DHA Phase 5, Karachi, we have grown into one of the city's most trusted names in power engineering — handling everything from diesel generator sales and rentals to solar energy systems, elevator installations, and comprehensive maintenance contracts.

Our team of experienced engineers and technicians brings decades of combined expertise, a commitment to using only genuine parts, and the dedication to be available 24/7 when our clients need us most.

A Decade of Growth

Company Founded
