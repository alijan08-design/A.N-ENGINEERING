Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our services, process, and support.

Generators

Generator Questions

What generator sizes do you supply?
We supply generators from 10kVA up to 2000kVA for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Both new and refurbished units are available from internationally recognized manufacturers. Our team will recommend the right size based on a proper load analysis of your premises.
Which generator brands do you carry?
We supply generators from leading brands including Cummins, Perkins, Caterpillar, Volvo, Kubota, Hino, and Isuzu. All units come with genuine documentation and warranty. We can also source specific brands or models on request.
Do you offer generator rental?
Yes, we offer flexible short and long-term generator rental from 10kVA to 2000kVA. Rental includes delivery, installation, and on-call technical support. We can also provide a qualified on-site operator if required. Daily, weekly, monthly, and long-term rates are available.
How long does generator installation take?
Standard generator installation (up to 500kVA) typically takes 3–7 working days depending on civil work requirements, ATS integration complexity, and site conditions. Larger installations may take up to 2–3 weeks. We provide a detailed project timeline before work begins.
Do you provide spare parts for generators?
Yes, we maintain a stock of genuine spare parts for all major generator brands. This includes filters, belts, injectors, batteries, alternator components, and more. We can also source specialized parts within 24–72 hours for less common models.
Solar Energy

Solar Questions

How much does a solar system cost in Karachi?
Solar system costs vary depending on size, panel brand, inverter type, and installation complexity. As a rough guide, residential systems typically range from PKR 3–8 lakh for a 5kW system to PKR 15–30 lakh for a 20–30kW commercial system. We provide a detailed, itemized quote after a free site survey.
Can I sell excess electricity back to K-Electric?
Yes, net metering allows you to export excess solar energy to the grid and receive a credit on your bill. A.N Power assists with the complete net metering application process with K-Electric, including system documentation, NEPRA approval, and bi-directional meter installation.
How long do solar panels last?
Quality solar panels typically have a 25–30 year performance warranty, maintaining over 80% of their rated output at end of life. Inverters generally have a 5–10 year lifespan. We only supply panels and inverters from reputable manufacturers with verifiable warranty terms.
Maintenance

Maintenance Questions

What does an Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) include?
Our AMC typically includes scheduled preventive maintenance visits (quarterly or bi-annual), oil and filter changes, battery checks, fuel system inspection, electrical safety testing, and a 24/7 emergency breakdown response. The exact scope is customized to your equipment and requirements.
How quickly can you respond to a breakdown?
For AMC clients, we target a 2–4 hour emergency response time within Karachi. For non-contract clients, we aim to respond within the same working day. We have on-call engineers available around the clock for critical situations.
Can you maintain equipment that you didn't install?
Yes, absolutely. We service and maintain generators, ATS panels, solar systems, and elevators regardless of who installed them, provided they are from brands we support. We perform an initial inspection to assess the equipment condition before signing an AMC.
