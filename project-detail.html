Case Study

500kVA Generator Installation

Commercial Complex — DHA Phase 6, Karachi

Generator Industrial Completed

Project Overview

This project involved the supply, delivery, installation, and commissioning of a 500kVA Cummins diesel generator for a large commercial complex in DHA Phase 6, Karachi. The client required a reliable standby power solution capable of supporting the entire building load — including lifts, HVAC, commercial units, and common area lighting — during KESC outages.

The project scope included civil foundation work, acoustic enclosure fabrication, a 500-litre sub-base fuel tank, automatic transfer switch panel integration, earthing system, and full load testing at site. The system was designed for an automatic switchover time of less than 10 seconds from utility failure.

The installation was completed within 12 working days, on schedule and within budget. A 2-year annual maintenance contract was also signed at project completion, ensuring the system remains in peak condition.

Challenges & Solutions

Limited Installation SpaceThe basement plant room had restricted access. We coordinated a custom crane lift to position the generator precisely, completing the placement in under 4 hours.
Complex Load SynchronizationExisting building electrical system required custom ATS programming to manage lift priority loads and prevent inrush current issues on startup.
